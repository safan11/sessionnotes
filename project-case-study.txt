Case Study 1: Digital Banking System (Core Banking Application)
--------------------------------------------------------------
Background
A bank was operating with branch-centric services where customers had to visit the bank for most activities like balance enquiry, fund transfer, or statement requests. This resulted in long queues, customer dissatisfaction, and heavy operational load on bank staff.

Problem
- Customers depended on branch visits for basic services
- Transactions were slow and time-bound
- Manual intervention increased errors

Solution
The bank introduced a Digital Core Banking System that allows customers to access banking services through online platforms from anywhere, at any time.

How It Works
- Customers log in securely using their credentials
- They can view account details, balances, and transaction history
- Funds can be transferred between accounts instantly
- All transactions are recorded centrally in real time

Outcome
- Banking services became available 24×7
- Reduced workload at bank branches
- Improved customer satisfaction and trust

Case Study 2: Loan Management System
-----------------------------------
Background
Loan processing in many banks and NBFCs was largely manual. Customers had to submit physical documents, and approvals took weeks due to multiple verification steps.

Problem
- Long loan approval time
- Manual document verification
- High chance of errors and inconsistent decisions

Solution
A Loan Management System was implemented to automate and streamline the entire loan lifecycle—from application to closure.

How It Works
- Customers apply for loans through an online form
- System validates customer details and eligibility
- EMI is calculated automatically
- Applications move through defined approval stages
- Customers receive real-time updates on loan status

Outcome
- Faster loan approvals
- Transparent process for customers
- Better control over loan risk
